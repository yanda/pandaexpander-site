Install in a minute

  1. Open the downloaded DMG and drag Panda Expander into Applications.
  2. Open Panda Expander. macOS may confirm that it was downloaded from the internet.
  3. Follow Getting Started and grant Accessibility access when prompted.
  4. Add the neutral examples or import your TextExpander CSV groups.
  5. Type ;hello in the built-in test field to verify expansion.

Your library is yours. Panda Expander starts empty and never ships another person’s snippets. Examples are optional and removable.

Updates

Panda Expander checks a signed Sparkle feed and offers new beta versions inside the app. You can also choose Check for Updates… at any time.

Something not working?

Open Panda Expander → Settings → Health and choose Copy Support Report. The report contains app, system, permission, and insertion metadata only—never snippet contents or surrounding text.
